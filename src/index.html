Our Mission, Vision, and Pillars

Mission ▶

Our mission is to elevate equity by empowering community-led initiatives that foster inclusive growth and sustainable development.

Vision ▶

Our vision is a thriving, equitable community where every individual has the opportunity to realize their full potential.

Pillars ▶

We Dig - Discover deep roots, beyond symptoms. We dig beneath the surface, even beyond our own assumptions to uncover the root causes that have kept inequities in place for generations.

We Center - Empower and trust those impacted. We center those who are directly impacted, honor their history, and TRUST their leadership.

We Imagine - Dream of an equitable world. We imagine the world as it should be and do not settle for the world as it is. A world of possibility & promise, equity & inclusion for all.

We Reflect - Review, improve, and stay responsible. We reflect in order to grow as individuals and organizations and to stay accountable to our values & our communities.
